� Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FuryFish Studio ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application Ant Smash (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy, especially the privacy of children.

1. Information Collection

✅ We do not collect any personal information.

"Personal Information" is information that identifies you as an individual, such as your name, email address, physical address, or telephone number.

We do not require you to provide any Personal Information to use our App. We do not ask for your location data.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our App is designed for children and families. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under the age of 13.
  • If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it.

3. Data Collected Automatically (Technical Data)

While we do not collect personal data, the App or third-party services we use may collect non-personal technical data to ensure the App functions correctly. This may include:

  • Device type and model.
  • Operating system version (e.g., iOS 18.0).
  • Crash logs (to help us fix bugs).

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for specific purposes. These services are restricted from using your data for any other purpose.

  • Apple Services: We use Apple's services to process payments for In-App Purchases. We do not have access to your credit card details.
  • Our Servers: If the app connects to our servers (e.g., to download game assets), we may log standard server access logs (like IP addresses) solely for security and performance monitoring. These logs are not linked to user profiles.

5. Camera Usage (Specific Apps Only)

Some of our applications (e.g., Emotion Cat) may require access to your device's camera to function (e.g., for facial expression detection).

If you use an application that requests Camera access, please note that:

  • No data storage: Camera data is processed locally on your device in real-time.
  • No transmission: Images or video from the camera are NEVER sent to our servers or third parties.
  • Ant Smash: This app does not access or use the camera.

6. TrueDepth API and Face Data

Our app Emo Kat uses the device's TrueDepth camera system (ARKit) to track the movement of your facial features. This allows you to interact with the virtual cat character using your facial expressions (e.g., smiling, blinking, or moving your head).

  • No data collection: We do not collect, store, or share any of your facial data.
  • Local processing only: All processing is performed locally on your device in real-time.
  • Immediate disposal: The depth data is used solely for the purpose of gameplay animation and is discarded immediately after use.
  • No transmission: We do not upload this data to any servers or share it with third parties.
